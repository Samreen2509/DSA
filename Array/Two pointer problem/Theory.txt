1) What is Two pointer?
Two pointer = Using 2 variables to scan and array/string

Instead of checking every element one by one, we check from both end at the same time.
Left pointer moves right
Right pointer moves Left

They meet in middle.
Cuts your work in half.

2) When to use?
  a) Array is sorted
  b) Question is asked to do in 0(n) time
  c) We are comparing element from both end
  d) We need to find pair or triplet
  e) Brute force lead nested loop
